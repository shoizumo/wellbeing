    About

    This is a visualization of the state of global Well-being. Well-being can be measured by three indexes which are 'Life Evaluation', 'Positive Experience Index' and 'Negative Experience Index'. This visualization provide Time-series change and comparison of these indexes and has 'Manual' and 'Auto' travel mode. While playing auto travel mode, we can know famous parson of each country.
